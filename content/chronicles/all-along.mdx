    Because it was a late night and my brain is generally running on empty, I decided to find a song that embodied the feeling I'm holding this evening and grab that as the name of today's post.

    <YouTubeVideo id="SyJaPv1Ad50" artist="kid cudi" album="the man on the moon ii: the legend of mr. rager" song="all along" />

    I don't normally post the fan fiction videos and would normally prefer just the static photo as opposed to fan art like this, but this particular song and this particlar creation will live with me forever.

    It's interesting to consider a day where so many nice things were said about me that when I reached into my feelings, this song, and it's message is what I reach for. In truth though, there's always been two ways I've always felt alone......

    First, inside of my own mind. Whenever I attempt to translate this feeling inside of me to the grander world around me, I'm almost always immediately met with silence. My perspective (my full perspective) is often so far out of left field that even when I get a response, it FEELS like silence. They didn't get it. They didn't hear me. How was I so misinterpreted?

    None of this to say that I consider myself particularly special or above anyone. Just different. For those that stick with me through the thick of it, I think I do a pretty good job seeing you for you. Maybe even better than you do sometimes. Without judgement. Acceptance. Togetherness.

    Which leads me to the second place I end up feeling so alone. I've got a wide net. My ability to support a wide ranging, often times rotating, cast of characters is one of these proverbial my strength ends up being my weakness type situations.

    There's an internal pressure I feel. Daily. Paradoxically, I feel very alone in these kinds of moments. The more I put myself out into the world, the more I see how much people need me, and the......
