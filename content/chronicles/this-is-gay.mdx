First, let's talk about randomness. If you refresh this page a few times you will see that the border & hashtag color will change (most of the time) to a random color on our beautiful spectrum.

Next, let's talk about random structure. The first thing the new color scheme does is randomly generate as many colors as there are sections. If you check out [yesterday's](/shaolin/hard-hat) page, there are 4 sections, and the first thing that chronicle does is generate 4 of the 7 colors of the rainbow. Then, no matter which 4 colors are generated, it orders them in the sequence of a classic rainbow.

Because of this, the first hashtag on [yesterday's](/shaolin/hard-hat) page (no border: remember, reasons) is more likely than not to be red. If you refresh enough times though, it ain't.

Random structure.
